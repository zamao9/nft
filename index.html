UNIVERSE

Through danger comes solidarity and through solidarity comes victory- They say

Deep in space, far from mankind, life prospers. But not life as we know it, a world populated by sentient robotic warriors. For thousands of years, they have been waging ruthless wars on spaceships and land driven by an insatiable appetite for domination, power and honor.

Only one day, Strangers emerged, bringing destruction and annihilation to their Galaxy. Facing this dark threat, the warriors joined forces and united as one brotherhood, one unstoppable Army. Bound to fight for their survival, they vowed to avenge their fallen brothers.

This cannot and will not be the end! An epic battle is coming.
