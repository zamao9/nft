How much does a SW cost?
The price is set at 0.2 ETH, however you should foresee a bit more for the gas fees.
Where can I view my NFTs?
Once minted, you'll just need to simply login into your OpenSea account to view your NFTs.
Will there be a pre-sale?
Yes, there will be pre-sales.
Spots are extremely limited, and only our whitelist members can join. To learn how to be part of our whitelist please join our Discord !
When am I going to be able to mint my Space Warrior?
The raffle registration starts on the 24th at 6pm UTC.
The whitelist sales will occur on the 25th at 6pm UTC.
The raffle sale will occur on the 25th at 8pm UTC.
How can I use my SW?
You will be able to use your NFT as an avatar in the metaverse as well as to access the first community-built videogame.
Your NFT also grants you access to vote for the future of the Space Warriors game, a game that YOU will build.
These elements give real value to your NFT and your investment.
What is a DAO?
A DAO is a Decentralised Autonomous Organisation serving a clearly defined function, a hierarchy-free governance system that empowers influence-free transactions, unbiased votes and more to be securely conducted.
DAOs enable a 100% transparent voting process in which anyone can access and review all vote data. In fact, as a proudly trustable venture, we actively encourage it!
How does Space Warrior Club use DAOs?
What is your strategy, warrior? The fate of our galaxy rests on your shoulders...
The Space Warriors Club uses a DAO structure to generate inputs from every community user in inspiring the trajectory of our community and game.
SWC DAO votes will be hosted on the Polygon Network, chosen for its effective harnessing of Ethereum Layer 2 technology and minimized gas fees for our community members.
Are there secondary sales royalties?
The royalties were fixed at 5% to fund the Space Warriors Club. We want to ensure the implementation of strong marketing campaigns and multiple events to strengthen the community, as well as ensure a high secondary market price. The fund collected will benefit the holders, the royalties can decrease over time.
What blockchain will you be using?
The Space Warriors will be hosted on the Ethereum blockchain.
Will there be giveaways?
Yes, there will be giveaways! You can check out our giveaway channel on Discord.
